Foundry VTT · Obojima 5e · Round 2 — full Ghibli warmth

A sheet that feels like a storybook page

Six warm, hand-painted directions — no more cold UI. Each is a full sheet for the same hero (Pim Hollowillow, Lv-5 Tideheart Witch) and leans on a different Ghibli still you shared: Totoro's forest, Howl's sky, the flower meadow, the bathhouse, a sunset sea, an ember-lit night. The big painted areas are drop-in art zones — that's where the magic lives, so drag your own scenes/portraits in and they'll carry the whole mood.
