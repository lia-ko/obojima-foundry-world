Foundry VTT · Obojima 5e · Character Sheet

Six sheet explorations

All six show the same character so you can compare directly — Pim Hollowillow, a level-5 Tideheart Witch. Each is framed as a Foundry window and varies along four axes: layout (single-page vs. tabbed), visual style, density (minimal → full crunch), and decorative framing. They're meant to be mixed and matched, not picked whole. Tabs, ability rolls, and the portrait drop-zones are live — try them.

01 · Parchment Classic 02 · Watercolor Tabs 03 · Tall-Grass Botanical 04 · Minimal Airy 05 · Storybook Spread 06 · Crunchy Console
Painted areas (portraits, spirit art, header washes) are drop-in placeholders — drag your own Obojima art onto them and it sticks. Drop reference screenshots into the project and I'll re-skin against the real book.
