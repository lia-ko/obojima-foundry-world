Story
Pim learned her brewing from a tide-witch on the southern reefs, trading tonics for stories. She wanders the tall grass with Mochi at her shoulder, mapping spirit-paths, bottling weather, and leaving every village a little better stocked than she found it. Allies: the Mistveil festival-keepers · the ferryman at Reed Crossing.
